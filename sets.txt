## **1. Personal Portfolio Website**

**Purpose:** Learn structure, semantic HTML, links, multimedia, forms, and accessibility.

### **Key Features to Implement**

* **Header & Navigation:**

  * `<header>`, `<nav>`
  * Internal links to sections (`<a href="#about">About</a>`)

* **About Section:**

  * Headings `<h1>`–`<h3>`
  * Paragraphs `<p>`
  * Images `<img>` with `alt`
  * Semantic `<section>` or `<article>`

* **Projects/Portfolio Gallery:**

  * Use `<figure>` and `<figcaption>` for each project
  * Optional `<iframe>` for embedded demos or videos

* **Skills List:**

  * Ordered or unordered lists `<ul>` / `<ol>`
  * Inline formatting `<strong>`, `<em>`

* **Contact Form:**

  * `<form>` with `<input>`, `<textarea>`, `<select>`, `<button>`
  * Proper labels `<label>` and `required` fields
  * Accessibility with ARIA roles

* **Footer:**

  * `<footer>` with social links and copyright

### **HTML Topics Covered**

* All basics, semantic HTML, links, images, multimedia, forms, accessibility, meta tags, and best practices.

## **2. Simple Blog / News Website**

**Purpose:** Deepen HTML5 skills with tables, multimedia, and more structured content.

### **Key Features to Implement**

* **Header & Navigation:**

  * Multiple pages linked via `<a>` tags
  * Navigation `<nav>` with dropdown links

* **Blog Articles:**

  * `<article>` for each post
  * `<section>` for categories
  * `<header>` inside articles for titles and `<footer>` for author/date

* **Tables:**

  * Display post statistics (views, comments) using `<table>`
  * Use `<thead>`, `<tbody>`, `<tfoot>` for clarity

* **Embedded Multimedia:**

  * Add `<video>` or `<audio>` in posts
  * Optional `<iframe>` for maps or external media

* **Comment Section / Form:**

  * `<form>` with text inputs, radio buttons, checkboxes, and submit button
  * Proper use of `<fieldset>` and `<legend>`

* **Sidebar / Widgets:**

  * `<aside>` for recent posts, tags, or ads
  * Semantic lists `<ul>`

* **Footer with Meta Information:**

  * `<footer>` containing meta links, social icons, and contact info

### **HTML Topics Covered**

* Advanced semantic HTML, multimedia, forms, tables, links, lists, meta info, and accessibility.

---

✅ **Tip:** For both projects, you can **add CSS later** for styling and **JavaScript for interactivity**,
 but focus first on **perfect, semantic, accessible HTML**.
